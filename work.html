Fashion Editorial

Still Water

A woman in a structured terracotta gown, alone in a woven boat on a perfectly still lake. Fog. Pampas grass. The reflection holds longer than the moment. Silence as a creative choice. Stillness as direction.

Elie Saab "Sketch to Reality"

A fashion illustration comes to life. The line between drawing and photograph dissolves. A couture gown — floral appliqué, trailing train — exists first as mark-making, then as fabric, then as both simultaneously.

Miu Miu "Unwrapped"

Miu Miu ballet flats wrapped in gold foil, like chocolate eggs — revealed as objects made to be consumed, not worn. One surreal gesture. One impossible object. Complete.

Massimo Dutti SS26

Massimo Dutti SS26 — a concept AI campaign at the intersection of brutalist architecture and the brand's quiet luxury. Real garments and footwear from the Massimo Dutti collection.

Yellow Series

A single color decision holds the whole series together: acid yellow against olive green. Linen, crocodile leather, gold hardware, an indifferent sea. Then the same yellow returns — traced along two eyes in a beauty triptych, proof that one color can carry a story across fashion and beauty both.

04

Playful Luxury

Jacquemus "Poireau/Maïs"

Croc-embossed clutches placed among olive trees, corn, and lavender — a Provençal still life where the bags exist as naturally as the wheat, as natural to the scene as anything grown there.

Villeroy & Boch — "Some Things Never Change"

An Easter campaign for a luxury tableware house. Two small girls in a sunlit room, taking tea from a miniature set. The porcelain pattern continues from their table to the adult table beyond. Heritage is learned at a small table, with small cups, long before anyone calls it heritage.

Sundae "Birthday Cake"

Sundae Birthday Cake Whipped Shower Foam. The product placed on swirled cream, surrounded by wafer biscuits, crowned on a pastel celebration cake, flanked by ice cream. The brief was simple: make the product look edible. The images made it look irresistible.

Jacquemus "Le Berlingot"

A Jacquemus Le Berlingot bag, suspended inside a claw machine. The machine is filled with pink berlingot candies — the same color as the bag itself. The concept is simple: desire is a game. The object you want most is always just out of reach — until it isn't.

Prada Spring Market Lisbon

An imaginary concept for Prada's Spring Market next pop-up: Lisbon. Praça do Comércio, red carnations, golden hour on Portuguese cobblestone — the same wooden crates and triangular bottles that traveled through Tokyo, Milan, and Madrid, now reimagined for a new city.
