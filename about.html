About

Julia Vaskul

I'm the founder of Basque Breeze, an AI visual production studio working across fashion, beauty, and commercial campaigns. I direct every project end to end — brand research, art direction, generation, edit — treating AI as a production tool rather than a shortcut.

Before this, I spent eleven years as a travel manager, and hold a Master's degree in Philology. I'm Ukrainian, based in Hendaye, on the French Basque coast, and work in Ukrainian, French, English, and Russian.

My aesthetic is cold minimalism: editorial eye, cinematic movement, the fashion object treated as the subject. Luxury tempo, intentional silence, and one impossible element per frame — never more.
