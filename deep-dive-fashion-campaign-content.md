## SECTION 4 — Production Pipeline
**Label:** ART DIRECTION
**Title (H2):** Production pipeline

**Body:**
> Building a stable AI model requires a disciplined creative approach. This ensures visual continuity and a fluid shift from the conceptual stage to the animation phase.

**Pipeline diagram (5 steps, horizontal, з фото під кожним):**
1. GENERATE — Midjourney, Nano Banana
2. POSE GRID — Nano Banana, Flux
3. ITERATE — Seedream, Nano Banana
4. UPSCALE — Magnific v2
5. ANIMATE — Kling

**Body:**
> From first prompt to final frame — every step was intentionally designed:

**Bullet list:**
- **GENERATE** — Face development (Midjourney + Nano Banana)
- **POSE BOARD** — Pose & angle exploration (Nano Banana + Flux)
- **ITERATE** — Full-body compositions in location (Seedream + Nano Banana)
- **UPSCALE** — Maximum detail & resolution (Magnific)
- **GRADE** — Cinematic color and light (Lightroom)
- **ANIMATE** — Subtle motion (Kling)

**Image:** портрет моделі крупним планом (обличчя, светр)

## SECTION 7 — Editorial Variants
**Label:** EDITORIAL VARIANTS
**Title (H2):** The impossible detail.

**Images (3, в ряд):**
1. Хода з відображенням у воді на заході сонця
2. Модель у круглому бетонному отворі (ч/б)
3. Модель сидить на чорному шкіряному дивані в бетонному приміщенні (кольоровий)

**Body:**
> Each Basque Breeze production includes one impossible detail — an element that cannot exist in a real photograph. Here: a sofa placed inside a raw concrete space. The domesticity of the chair against the institutional weight of the wall. The body at rest inside the architecture of resistance.
>
> This is the signature element that transforms an editorial into a campaign image. It is not decoration. It is the idea.
